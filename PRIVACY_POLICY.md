# Privacy Policy for Bahria LMS Assignment Tracker

**Effective Date:** November 30, 2025

## Data Collection and Usage

Bahria LMS Assignment Tracker ("the Extension") is designed to help students track their assignments from lms.bahria.edu.pk.

### What Data We Collect

- **Assignment Information**: The extension scrapes assignment titles, deadlines, course names, and submission status from your LMS assignments page.
- **Local Storage**: All assignment data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

### How We Use Your Data

- To display your pending assignments in the extension popup
- To send you reminder notifications for upcoming deadlines (3 days, 2 days, and on the due date)
- To track which notifications have been shown to avoid duplicates

### Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API
- No personal information, login credentials, or assignment data is sent to any external servers
- No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used

### Permissions Used

- **storage**: To save your assignments locally
- **alarms**: To schedule reminder notifications
- **notifications**: To show deadline reminders
- **tabs**: To interact with LMS pages for assignment collection
- **scripting/activeTab**: To read assignment data from lms.bahria.edu.pk
- **host_permissions (lms.bahria.edu.pk)**: To access and scrape assignment information from your LMS

### Data Sharing

We do **not**:

- Share your data with any third parties
- Transmit your data to external servers
- Track your browsing activity
- Collect any personally identifiable information

### Data Retention

- Assignment data is retained until you manually sync/refresh or uninstall the extension
- You can clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension

### Your Rights

You can:

- View your stored assignments anytime through the extension popup
- Clear your data by uninstalling the extension
- Control notification permissions through your browser settings

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's listing on the Chrome Web Store.
